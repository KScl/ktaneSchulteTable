On the Subject of Cruel Schulte Table

Just press all the encrypted letters in order. Encrypted...?

The module presents a 5 by 5 grid and a screen.

Press any square in the grid to initiate a 300-second timer. The grid will also display encrypted letters from A to Y.

To solve the module, press each letter in alphabetical order if the last digit of the serial number is even, otherwise press each letter in reverse alphabetical order.

Pressing a wrong letter will yield a strike and reset the module. Letting the timer run out will not yield a strike, but still reset the module.

The list on the bottom of this page and next page contains all encryptions that will be used in the module. They may aid you in the decryption process.
